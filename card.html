Peacock

The large iridescent train (tail) is on full display to attract the peahens during breeding season.

  • Scientific Name: Afropavo, Pavo
  • Type: Birds
  • Diet: Omnivores
  • Habitat: Forests and rainforests

The distinctive train (tail) containing over 200 shimmering feathers, of vibrant colours, covers more than 60 percent of the bird's total body length. With a wingspan of around 1.5 metres, the peacock remains one of the largest flying birds on earth.
